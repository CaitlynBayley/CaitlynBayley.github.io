What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self-awareness stems from a study of emotional awareness. By observing our emotions in different situations, we can startto understand the way they behave. From creating this space we can objectively observe our emotions, as we do our thoughts during mindfulness. We observe the emotion, we accept the emotion and we can let go of the emotion if we understand it doesn't define us or the situation we are in. To develop a strong emotional awareness, can lead to a more accurate self-assessment, which gives us the tools to increase our own self confidence.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I really enjoyed this last section! It is a thought I have had before and typically it leads me down a path where I end up having an existential crisis. But it is so true, we are not our emotions or our thoughts. My emotions drive and define a lot of my experiences on a day-to-day basis, and strong common emotions and ways of thinking are central to many of my friendships. It is a good reminder that while they may be "true", they come and go, constantly shifting. To acknowledge them and their impact while knowing they will pass is a very liberating and terrifying thought...
